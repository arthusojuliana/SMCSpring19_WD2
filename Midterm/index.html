About Us

Lit-er-a-ti

(lit'-er-ah'-tee) pl. n

1. The literary intelligentsia
2. A counter service cafe offering organic coffee & tea, fresh salads, sandwiches, soups, entrees and pastries.

Have you noticed that the only constant in life, is change?
True to the laws of the universe, Literati Café continues to fine tune our offerings and listen to the needs and wants of our customers. Our menu has been updated, full service is available, and the café, along with our patio, have touches to ensure your visit is satisfying.
